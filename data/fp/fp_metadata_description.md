# A Short Note on Dataset Labeling

Note: a particular label for a video may be `S00027_feeding1_2`. The `S` represents the subject is a stroke patient (rather than a `C` for a control subject). The `00027` is the subject's identifier. `feeding` is the activity in the video, and `1_2` mean that this is the subject's first repetition of the activity, filmed from camera angle `2` (there are two possible camera angles).
